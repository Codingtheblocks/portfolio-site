A modern cryptocurrency trading dashboard UI blending dark neon aesthetics of Raydium, the clean data grids of GeckoTerminal, the minimalist layout of Gains.Trade, and the modular pane system of TradeStation. The interface features a dark background with subtle gradients and glowing accent colors (blues, purples, cyans). Top navbar with branding and theme toggle. Left sidebar showing a vertical watchlist of tokens with price change and volume info. Center panel displays a real-time candlestick chart with overlays and a strategy control toolbar beneath it. Right panel shows recent trades, active bots, and account balance. Bottom section includes a strategy builder with toggles, dropdowns, and a start/backtest button. Overall layout uses clean cards, soft shadows, rounded corners (2xl), and readable sans-serif fonts. Hover tooltips, minimal animations, and clear hierarchy. Inspired by fintech dashboards like TradingView, DeFi apps, and algorithmic trading platforms.
